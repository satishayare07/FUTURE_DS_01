Task 1: Superstore Sales Performance Analysis

Objective:
The objective of this task is to analyze the sales performance of a Superstore using Power BI and identify key business insights related to revenue, profit, regions, categories, and monthly trends.

Dataset Used:
Superstore Sales Dataset

Tools Used:
• Microsoft Power BI
• Microsoft Excel

Key KPIs:
• Total Revenue: 1.57M
• Total Profit: 175.26K
• Average Order Value (AOV): 521.41

Key Insights:
1. The West region generated the highest revenue (~0.52M), followed by the East region (~0.45M), indicating strong market performance in these areas.
2. Technology category contributes the highest share of revenue (~470.59K), making it the top-performing category.
3. Revenue shows an increasing trend towards the end of the year, with December recording the highest monthly sales (~245K).
4. Products like Canon ImageCLASS and 3D Systems Cube Printer are among the top revenue-generating products.
5. The South region has the lowest revenue contribution, highlighting an opportunity for targeted marketing and sales strategies.

Conclusion:
This dashboard provides a clear overview of sales performance across regions, categories, and time, helping stakeholders make data-driven business decisions.
