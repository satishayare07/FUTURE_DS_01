Task 3: Marketing Funnel & Conversion Performance Analysis

Objective:
The objective of this task is to analyze the marketing funnel and conversion performance to understand user behavior from browsing to purchase and evaluate channel-wise and device-wise performance.

Dataset Used:
Marketing Funnel Dataset

Tools Used:
• Microsoft Power BI
• Microsoft Excel

Key KPIs:
• Total Users: 10K
• Total Sessions: 10K
• Total Purchases: 1004
• Total Revenue: 277.32K
• Conversion Rate: 10.0%

Key Insights:
1. Out of 10K users, only 1004 users completed purchases, resulting in a 10% conversion rate.
2. The biggest drop-off occurs between the Add to Cart and Purchase stages, indicating checkout friction.
3. Desktop users contribute the highest number of sessions compared to Mobile and Tablet.
4. Google Ads and Social Media are the top-performing marketing channels in terms of purchases.
5. Electronics category generates the highest revenue among all product categories.

Conclusion:
The dashboard highlights opportunities to improve checkout experience and optimize high-performing channels to increase conversions and revenue.
