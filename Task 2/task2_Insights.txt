Task 2: Customer Retention & Churn Analysis

Objective:
The objective of this task is to analyze customer churn and retention patterns based on demographics, tenure, activity status, and product usage.

Dataset Used:
Customer Churn Dataset

Tools Used:
• Microsoft Power BI
• Microsoft Excel

Key KPIs:
• Total Customers: 10K
• Total Exited Customers: 2037
• Churn Rate: 20.4%
• Retention Rate: 79.6%
• Average Tenure: 5.0 years

Key Insights:
1. Overall churn rate is 20.4%, meaning nearly one-fifth of customers have exited.
2. Customers with lower tenure show higher churn rates, indicating early-stage dissatisfaction.
3. Inactive customers have significantly higher churn compared to active customers.
4. Male customers show slightly higher churn compared to female customers.
5. Customers using fewer products are more likely to churn than those using multiple products.

Conclusion:
Improving customer engagement, increasing product adoption, and focusing on early tenure customers can help reduce churn and improve retention.
